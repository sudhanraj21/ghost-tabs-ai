GhostTabs AI - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Overview

GhostTabs AI is a Chrome extension that helps users manage browser tabs by converting inactive tabs to lightweight ghost tabs. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

GhostTabs AI stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Tab URLs and Titles: For identifying and restoring parked tabs
  • User Settings: Your preferences (language, auto-park settings, excluded domains)
  • Tab Activity: Timestamps to determine when tabs become inactive

We do NOT collect: Personal identifiable information, health data, financial data, authentication credentials, location data, or browsing history beyond tab management.

Data Usage

All collected data is used solely for the core functionality of GhostTabs:

  • Auto-parking inactive tabs
  • Restoring ghost tabs on demand
  • Managing tab categories (Watch, Learn, Read, Buy, Work)
  • Providing settings and preferences

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, transfer, or share any user data with third parties. All data remains on your local device.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API. Users can delete all stored data by uninstalling the extension or clearing browser data.

Contact

For privacy concerns, contact through the Chrome Web Store listing.

Compliance

This extension complies with Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies and applicable data protection regulations.
